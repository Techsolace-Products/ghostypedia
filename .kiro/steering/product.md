# Product Overview

Ghostypedia is an immersive, AI-powered encyclopedia of ghosts, creatures, myths, and paranormal entities. It blends storytelling, knowledge exploration, and personalized recommendations into one spooky, engaging platform.
The project is currently in its initial setup phase, where core structure, user flow, and content models are being established.

## Purpose
Ghostypedia aims to become the ultimate digital hub for ghost enthusiasts, horror fans, and storytelling lovers.
Its core purpose is to:
Provide a rich, searchable knowledge base of ghost types, folklore, and paranormal entities.
Offer personalized recommendations for movies, stories, myths, and paranormal content based on user preferences.
Allow users to interact with their own AI-powered digital twin, making the experience more immersive and customized.
Serve as an engaging platform for discovering spooky content in a fun, modern way.
As the project evolves, features like interactive stories, community submissions, and advanced personalization will take shape.
